Application

Applicants for admission to Hearthstone are interviewed at house meetings held from 6 p.m. to 7 p.m. on Monday through Thursday evenings.  In order to be interviewed, applicants must:
  • Not have used drugs or alcohol within 72 hours of the interview
  • Be able to move in within 72 hours if accepted
It is advisable to:
  • Call first in order to determine availability
  • Arrive between 5:00 and 5:30 on the designated day in order to have the opportunity to become more familiar with Hearthstone

Applicants are interviewed on a first-come, first-served basis and no waiting list is maintained.  If someone is accepted but does not move in within 72 hours, the opening is made available to others and he must re-interview.

If Hearthstone is at its capacity when someone calls, he is encouraged to call back frequently as there is no way to predict when openings will become available.
